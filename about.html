Our Story

Vallarta's first location belongs to the City of Temple Terrace, which opened on July 9th, 1992. The first founder of Vallarta's is Jose Jimenez Macias, along with his wife, Maria C. Jimene Leon, and his cousins from Georgia, Juan Manuel Onate Macias and Jose Antonio Onate Macias. Vallarta's is a family owned and operated business. Because of the first location's success, Jose started to open up more locations throughout Tampa Bay, which gave his family the opportunity to partner up with him in the restaurant business.
